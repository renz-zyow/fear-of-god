Projects

Essentials, as its name implies, produces collections of simple pieces in muted tones. Where F.O.G. was basically a more affordable version of Fear of God, Essentials was Lorenzo’s attempt to properly segment his creations. Since its launch Essentials has continued to flourish, and as streetwear fans can attest, it’s the best way for them to get their hand on Lorenzo’s pieces.

ESSENTIALS

The Essentials short sleeve tee is elevated in a 380-gram heavy cotton jersey to provide pronounced weight and structure. The rib-knit crewneck fits relaxed in the body with dropped shoulders. A collegiate-inspired Essentials Fear of God logo on the center front is made modern in a rubberized soft-touch texture. An Essentials Fear of God rubberized label is at the back collar.

Basics, Hoodies, Tees, streetwear

ADIDAS X FEAR OF GOD

The Fear of God Athletics Suede Fleece Pant is elevated in a heavy double-knit to provide structure and comfort with a rich, brushed texture. Signature adidas 3-Stripe branded side panels frame the relaxed, straight-leg fit silhouette with a pintuck seam to add definition. Functional details include an elastic waistband with drawcord, side seam pockets with perforated 4-way stretch lining, and side zippers at the ankle. A sueded black bar label on the center front defines minimalist branding.

FEAR OF GOD

The stand collar overcoat is tailored in Italy from boiled virgin wool to provide a natural texture, complemented by iridescent cupro lining. The minimalist design is clean, without any closures or hardware. Volume in the body allows comfortable layering with a fashionable drape. A self-fabric waist belt, chest welt pockets, and side pockets offer functionality. A leather Fear of God label is stitched on the sleeve.
